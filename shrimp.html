Shrimp

Shrimp are small, marine or freshwater crustaceans characterized by their elongated bodies, multiple pairs of legs, and a curved, fan-like tail. They are a popular seafood choice known for their tender and sweet meat. Shrimp come in various species and sizes, with culinary applications ranging from shrimp cocktails to stir-fries and scampi. They are an essential component of many cuisines worldwide and are often harvested through commercial fishing and aquaculture. Shrimp are a versatile and delicious ingredient in a wide range of dishes.
