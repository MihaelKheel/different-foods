Ice Cream

Ice cream is a frozen dessert enjoyed worldwide. It's typically made from a mixture of cream, milk, sugar, and flavorings, which are churned and frozen to create a smooth and creamy texture. It comes in a wide variety of flavors, ranging from classic vanilla and chocolate to more exotic options like mint chocolate chip or strawberry cheesecake. Ice cream is often served in cones, cups, or as a topping for various desserts, making it a beloved treat for people of all ages.
